SOME TIPS TO BUILD YOURSELF CONFIDENCE


  1. DRESS SHARP

    2. Although clothes don't make the man, they certainly affect the way he feels about himself. No one is more concious of your physical appearance then you are. When you don't look good, it changes the way you carry yourself and interact with other people. Use your advantaage by taking care ofyour personal appearance. In most cases, significant improvements can be made by bathing and shaving frequently, wearing clean clothes

  2. WALK FASTER

    3. One of the easiest way to tell how a person feels about himself is to examine his walk. is to slow? tired? painful? or is it energetic and purposeful? people with confidence walk quickly. they have places to go, people to see, and important work to do. Even if you aren't in a hurry you can increase your self-confidence by putting some preparation in your step. Walking 25% faster will make you feel look and more important.

  3. GOOD POSTURE

    4. people with slumped shoulders and lethargic movements display a lack of self-confidence. they aren't enthustiatic about what they are doing and they don't consider themselves important. By Practicing good posture, you'll automatically feel more confident. standup straight keep your head up, and make eye contact. you will make a positive impression on others and instantly feel more alert and improved.

  4. Personal commercial

    5. One of the best ways to build confidence is listening to a motivational speech. Unfortunately, opportunitiesto listen to a great speaker are few and far between. you can fill this need by creating a personal commercial. write a 30-60 second speech that highlights your strengths and your goals. then recite infront of the mirror aloud(or inside your head if you prefer) whenever you need a cofidence boost.

  5. Gratitude

    6. When you focus too much on what you wnat the mind creates reasons why you can't have it. This leads you to dwell on your weaknesses the best way to avoide this is conciously focusing on gratitude. Set aside time each day to mentslly list everything you have to be grateful for. Recall your passt success , unique skills, loving relationships and positive momentum you'll be amazed how much you have going for you and motivated to take that next step towards success.

  6. Compliment Other people

    7. when we think negatively about overselves, we often project that feeling on to others in the forms of insults and gossip. To break this cycle of negativity, get inthe habbit of praising other people. Refuse to engage in bsckstabing gossip and make effort to complete those around you. In this process, you will become well liked and build self-confidence. By looking for the best in others, you indirectly bring out the best in yourself

  7. Sit In The Front Row

    8. in schools, offices and public assemblies around the world, people constantly strive to sit at the back of the room. Most people prefer the back because they're afraid of being noticed. This reflects a lack of self-confidence. By deciding to sit in the front of the row, you can get over this irational fear and build your self-confidence. You'll also be more visible to the important people talking from front of the room.

  8. Speak Up

    9. During group discussion, many people never speak up because they afraid that people will judge them for saying something stupid. This fear isn't really justified Generally, people are much more accepting than we imagine. In fact, most people are dealing with the exact same fears. by making an effort to speak at least once in every group discussion, you'll become a better public speaker , more confident in your own thoughts, and recognised as a leader by your peers.

  9. Work Out

    10. Along the same lines as personal appearance, physical fitness has a huge efect on self-confidence. If you're out of shape , you'll feel insecure, unattractive and less energetic. By working out , you improve your physical appearance, energise yourself, and accomplish something positive. Having the discipline to work out not only makes you feel better, it creates psitive momentum that you can build on the rest of day.
